BC Powder

BC Powder is an over-the-counter pain relief medication that combines aspirin and caffeine. It is commonly used to treat headaches, body aches, and other mild to moderate pain symptoms.

What is BC Powder used for?

BC Powder can be used to treat:

  • Headaches
  • Body aches
  • Minor arthritis pain
  • Toothaches
It is known for its fast-acting relief due to its powder form.

Types of BC Powder

BC Powder is available in:

  • Original Formula
  • Arthritis Formula

How to take BC Powder

BC Powder should be dissolved in water or taken directly, followed by a liquid. Adults can take one powder every 4 to 6 hours as needed. Do not exceed 4 powders in 24 hours.

Possible side effects

Side effects may include:

  • Upset stomach
  • Heartburn
  • Increased risk of bleeding (due to aspirin)
If side effects worsen, discontinue use and consult a doctor.

Warnings and precautions

Consult a doctor before taking BC Powder if you have:

  • Stomach ulcers or bleeding disorders
  • Heart problems or high blood pressure
Avoid alcohol while taking BC Powder as it may increase the risk of stomach bleeding.
