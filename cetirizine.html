Cetirizine

Cetirizine is an antihistamine used to treat allergy symptoms like sneezing, runny nose, and itching. It is also used for hay fever and hives.

What is cetirizine used for?

Cetirizine is used to relieve:

  • Hay fever symptoms
  • Allergic rhinitis
  • Chronic urticaria (hives)
It works by blocking histamines that cause allergic reactions.

Types of cetirizine

Cetirizine is available in:

  • Tablets
  • Liquid syrup (for children)
  • Disintegrating tablets

How to take cetirizine

Adults and children over 12 can typically take 10mg once a day. For children aged 6-12, the dose is usually 5mg twice a day.

Possible side effects

Side effects can include:

  • Drowsiness
  • Dry mouth
  • Headaches
  • Fatigue

Warnings and precautions

Consult a doctor before taking cetirizine if you have:

  • Kidney problems
  • Liver issues
