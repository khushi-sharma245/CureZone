Combiflam

Combiflam is a combination of ibuprofen and paracetamol, used for pain relief, inflammation, and fever reduction.

What is Combiflam used for?

Combiflam can be used for:

  • Headaches
  • Muscle pain
  • Fever
  • Dental pain
It is often used to relieve mild to moderate pain and inflammation.

Types of Combiflam

Combiflam is available in:

  • Tablets
  • Syrup (for children)

Possible side effects

Side effects from Combiflam can include:

  • Nausea
  • Stomach upset
  • Heartburn

Warnings and precautions

Combiflam should be used cautiously in people with:

  • Stomach ulcers
  • Asthma
  • Liver or kidney disease
